About the Platform

CivicConnect is a web platform designed to make it easier for citizens to communicate with their elected representatives. It allows users to report issues, provide feedback, and receive updates directly from politicians. The goal is to improve transparency, trust, and responsiveness in governance.

Every interaction on the platform is moderated and monitored to maintain respect and constructive dialogue between all parties.

User Roles

Admin

Oversees platform operations, manages user roles, and ensures data integrity.

Citizen

Reports issues, provides feedback, and receives updates from politicians.

Politician

Responds to citizen concerns, posts updates, and engages in discussions.

Moderator

Monitors interactions, ensures respectful dialogue, and upholds community standards.
